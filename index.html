Welcome to gloceps Global Policy Center for Policy and Strategy

s a non-profit Organization based in Nairobi, Kenya. The Centre was founded in 2020 and has an overarching vision of being a leading global Centre of excellence in action research, influential policy advisory and strategy formulation. We are a pioneer institution of excellence that provides a strategic link between experience and inter-disciplinary research on one end and policy formulation and action on the other.
